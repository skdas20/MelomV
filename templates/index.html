About Melometeo

Our Story

Melometeo began as a simple idea in the mind of Sumit Kumar Das. Fascinated by the relationship between emotions, environment, and music, he envisioned a system that could understand these connections and create personalized musical experiences.

What started as a mere concept soon transformed into reality through Sumit's dedication and perseverance. With countless hours of research, coding, and refining algorithms, he breathed life into this project, creating a unique platform that bridges technology and human emotion.

Today, Melometeo stands as a testament to innovation, combining artificial intelligence, weather data, and facial recognition to create a truly personalized music recommendation system that adapts to who you are and how you feel.

"Music has the power to transform our state of mind. My vision was to create a system that understands this connection and enhances it by adapting to each individual's unique circumstances."

- Sumit Kumar Das, Creator

How It Works

1

Capture Your Mood

Our AI-powered computer vision system analyzes your facial expressions in real-time to determine your current emotional state, from happy and energetic to calm and reflective.

2

Analyze Environment

We detect your location and gather real-time weather data. Whether it's a sunny day, a rainy evening, or a snowy morning, weather influences our musical preferences in subtle ways.

3

Detect Age Group

Our system estimates your age range to customize recommendations that align with generational music preferences and nostalgic connections.

4

Process Factors

Our proprietary algorithm combines these three key factors—mood, weather, and age—to create a unique musical fingerprint that represents your current state and context.

5

Generate Playlist

Using this fingerprint, we search through millions of tracks to curate a personalized playlist that resonates with you in this specific moment, enhancing your emotional experience.
